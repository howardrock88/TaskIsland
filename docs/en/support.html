TaskIsland Support

If you run into an issue or want to share feedback, please contact the developer through GitHub Issues.

Helpful details to include

  • Your macOS version.
  • Your TaskIsland version.
  • The steps that led to the issue.
  • Screenshots or a screen recording, if available.

TaskIsland is currently local-first. No account is required, and task data is stored on your Mac by default.
