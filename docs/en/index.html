TaskIsland

TaskIsland is a local-first floating task manager for macOS. It keeps your tasks, reminders, and focus timer visible at the top of your desktop.

  • Floating island: See task counts, active focus, and priority tasks at a glance.
  • Focus timer: Start, pause, and complete focus sessions without switching windows.
  • Local-first: No account is required, and task data is stored on your Mac by default.
